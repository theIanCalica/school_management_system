Brigada Eskwela Best Implementer Award

Abra de Ilog Central School Takes Home 3rd Place in Brigada Eskwela Best Implementer Award!

Abra de Ilog Central School is beaming with pride today! The school has recently been awarded 3rd place in the prestigious Brigada Eskwela Best Implementer Award, a testament to the dedication and hard work of the entire school community in preparing for the new school year.

Brigada Eskwela is a nationwide DepEd program that encouraages parents, teachers, students, and other stakeholders to come together and participate in cleaning, repairing, and beautifying schools before the start of the academic year. This year's theme, "Brigada Eskwela: Pagkakaisa para sa ligtas, maayos at handang paaralan," emphasized the importance of collaboration and unity in creating a safe, conducive, and inspiring learning environment for students.

Abra de Ilog Central School truly embodied the spirit of Brigada Eskwela. From students diligently painting murals and cleaning classrooms to parents and teachers working tirelessly on repairs and maintenance, the entire community rolled up their sleeves and contributed in their own way. The school also partnered with local businesses and organizations to secure donations of paint, construction materials, and other essential supplies.
