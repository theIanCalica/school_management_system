District Meet


District Meet

Mark your calendars on March 2024!

Get ready to roar, ACS Tigers! On March , our fiercest athletes will take on the competition at the District Track & Field Meet. Dust off your sneakers, sharpen your spikes, and prepare to unleash your sportsmanship on the track! Let your speed, agility, and strength shine. Whether you're a sprinting gazelle, a hurdling panther, or a high-jumping, every roar of the crowd will fuel your fire.

Come out and support our Tigers as they chase gold, break records, and leave their paw prints on the district stage. Cheer on your classmates, friends, and neighbors as they showcase their dedication and sportsmanship. Admission is free, so gather your roars and pack your school spirit! Don't miss this chance to witness Central School pride take flight!
