Abra De Ilog Central School

Abra De Ilog Central School is situated in an urban location in Poblacion, Abra de Ilog, Occidental Mindoro. Established in 1910, it is recognized for its commitment to educational excellence and serves as a comprehensive hub of learning, fostering holistic student development.
More About Us

Abra De Ilog Central School features cutting-edge facilities, including modern science and computer labs, technologically advanced classrooms, a spacious multipurpose hall, and resourceful libraries.

The institution offers playgrounds, a well-equipped gym, and an auditorium for diverse activities. Essential services like a dedicated clinic ensure health and safety. A dynamic student lounge fosters relaxation and socialization, promoting holistic student development.
